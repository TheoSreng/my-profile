Volleyball

How it all started

I started playing volleyball after watching Haikyuu!! a Japanese TV show. I went to a club in 2019 and from this point I've never stopped playing and practicing. It was the first sport I really was hooked on. Even during the pandemic crisis of the corona virus, I practiced at home working on my vertical jump. This activity really had a great impact on me: I developped a great team spirit and it helpped me lose a lot of weight.

The players I admire

Yuji Nishida

Yuji Nishida is a Japanese Opposite that is 1.86m which is relatively small compared to other players playing the same role. He was born the 30 of january 2000 and started his international career at the age of 18. Despite being one of the youngest player in the 2018 roster of the Japan men's national team, he definitely imposed himself as a star player. He played a crucial role in securing Japan's first win in 11 years against Italy where he collected 24 points. In 2018, year of his debut as an international player, he became the youngest player ever to score 30 points in a match. Later, in 2019, he broke serving records in the VNL history after hitting 7 aces in a single game during a match against Canada.

Yuki Ishikawa

Yuki Ishikawa is also a Japanese player, he is an outside spiker and measures 1.92m. He was born the 11 of December 1995 so he is now 27 years old. Ishikawa was named "Best Outside spiker" award at the FIVB World Cup 2015 and 2019; asian Championship 2017, 2019 and 2021; as well as "Most valuable player" award at the Asian Championship 2017. Ishikawa is the captain of the Japan men's national volleyball team and plays in the italian Volleyball League, one of the best League, for Allianz Milano.
